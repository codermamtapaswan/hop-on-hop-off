Overview

This hop-on hop-off sightseeing bus tour is the ultimate convenient way of exploring London's top landmarks. Avoid the stress of navigating London's underground system, and instead—simply hop on and off the bus at stops on its designated route. Potential stops include Buckingham Palace, the Tower of London, Big Ben, Oxford Street, and The Shard.

When does it run? Daily
Duration 24 or 48 hours (take your pick)
Meeting Point Hop-On, Hop-Off: You can board the bus at any of the route's stops. The favored stops are Eiffel Tower, The Louvre, Champs-Elysées, Notre-Dame, and Opéra Garnier
Start time Hop-On Hop-Off Tour: 9:45am to 5:30pm, every 10 minutes. (After 5pm, our buses will no longer complete a full tour)
Cruise Make your way to Pontoon No. 3, Port de la Bourdonnais (near Stop #8: Eiffel Tower)
Ending Point You can end the hop-on hop-off bus tour at any of the stops.
Redemption Info
  • You can board the bus at any stop and voucher can be exchanged directly with the driver.
  • Mobile and paper vouchers are both accepted.
  • Voucher : You will receive a ticket in exchange for your voucher, which you must keep until the expiry date/ tour.
  • Ticket Expiry : Please check your ticket to know when it expires.
  • Ticket Exchange : After exchanging your voucher for a ticket, your voucher will no longer be valid.
  • Ticket Loss Policy : In loss of voucher/ticket, company is not liable for any refund.
  • Flexible Ticket : The voucher can be used within 5 days from the date indicated during the purchase.
  • Operational Updates : Starting from tomorrow, July 03rd to July 27th, stop no. 11 of Line A will be suspended.
