    This hop-on hop-off sightseeing bus tour is the ultimate convenient way of exploring London's top landmarks. Avoid the stress of navigating London's underground system, and instead—simply hop on and off the bus at stops on its designated route. Potential stops include Buckingham Palace, the Tower of London, Big Ben, Oxford Street, and The Shard.

    The Paris Hop On Hop off Must See pass is the perfect way to explore the City of Lights on foot and by boat. Not only can you easily hop on and off at any stop, but our eco-friendly buses also have open-air top decks that provide an unforgettable ride with breathtaking views of Paris from a height of four meters.

    During your tour, you'll have the chance to see some of the most famous landmarks in Paris, including the Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Orsay Museum, Champs-Elysées, Arc de Triomphe, Eiffel Tower, Les Invalides, and more.

    To complete your Parisian experience, we offer a Vedettes de Paris Seine River cruise as part of your ticket. The pier is conveniently located near stop #9, at the "Port de Suffren" on the riverbanks beneath the Eiffel Tower, and is the perfect way to cap off your trip.

    Join us on our "A new look at Paris" guided cruise for an unusual tour of the city and its bridges, leaving from the Eiffel Tower and passing via Notre-Dame and the Seine's famed jewels. Enjoy a cup of coffee, a cookie, or a snack while you're on the water.
